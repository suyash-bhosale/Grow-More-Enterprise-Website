Our Services

EVI

EVI (Enhanced Vegetation Index) monitors the health and status of plants. It is especially useful for tracking vegetation greenness.

MSI

MSI (Moisture Stress Index) assesses moisture stress in crops, helping farmers take preventive measures based on water stress.

SAVI

SAVI (Soil Adjusted Vegetation Index) analyzes vegetation growth over bare soil, helping to assess crop health based on soil conditions.
