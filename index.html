What are EVI & MSI?

🌱 EVI (Enhanced Vegetation Index)

EVI helps assess plant vigor and chlorophyll activity...

  • Low EVI (below 0.2): Poor crop or barren land.
  • Moderate EVI (0.2 - 0.4): Stressed crops.
  • Good EVI (0.4 - 0.6): Healthy growth.
  • High EVI (above 0.6): Excellent condition.

💧 MSI (Moisture Stress Index)

MSI identifies areas where plants are experiencing moisture shortage...

  • MSI below 0.8: Moisture is sufficient.
  • MSI between 0.8 - 1.2: Moderate stress.
  • MSI above 1.2: High stress - irrigate immediately.
